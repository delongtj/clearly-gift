## Choosing the Perfect Gift for Teachers

When selecting gifts for the teacher in your life, think about what would make their daily routine better:

- **Everyday essentials**: YETI tumbler, Pilot G2 pens, and notebooks are used daily and always appreciated
- **Self-care**: Burt's Bees set, Ember Mug, and food delivery cards remind them to take care of themselves
- **Professional tools**: Erin Condren planner and AirPods support their teaching workflow
- **Flexibility**: Gift cards let them choose exactly what they need most right now

## Pro Tips for Gifting Teachers

1. **Skip the mugs**: Teachers have dozens of "Best Teacher" mugs already (unless it's an Ember Mug!)
2. **Gift cards are NOT impersonal**: They're practical and respectful of a teacher's preferences
3. **Consider timing**: End-of-year and holiday gifts are common, but a surprise mid-year gift hits differently
4. **Group gifts go further**: Rally other parents to contribute to one meaningful gift instead of 25 small ones
5. **Include a heartfelt note**: The most treasured teacher gifts often aren't the item—they're the words of appreciation that come with it

Teachers give so much of themselves every day. The right gift says "I see you, I appreciate you, and I want to make your life a little easier."
