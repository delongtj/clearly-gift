## Choosing the Perfect Gift for New Parents

When selecting gifts for new parents, think about what will actually make their day-to-day life easier:

- **Practical over cute**: Adorable outfits are lovely, but babies outgrow them in weeks. Gear that solves a daily problem gets used for months or years.
- **Self-care matters**: Parents often forget to take care of themselves. Gifts like the Ember Mug or Keurig remind them they deserve nice things too.
- **Don't forget the non-birthing parent**: Many gifts are marketed toward moms, but dads and partners are in the trenches too. Choose gifts both parents can enjoy.
- **Consider the stage**: Newborn needs are different from 6-month needs. If the baby is already here, ask what they're currently struggling with.

## Pro Tips for Gifting New Parents

1. **Skip the newborn-size clothes**: Babies often arrive bigger than expected, and newborn sizes last days, not weeks
2. **Gift cards are not lazy—they're brilliant**: Let parents choose what they actually need from stores like Target or Amazon
3. **Meal delivery is gold**: A DoorDash or Uber Eats gift card can be more meaningful than any physical product
4. **Offer your time**: Sometimes the best gift is offering to hold the baby so they can nap, shower, or just sit in silence
5. **Group gifts work**: Rally friends and family to chip in on one big-ticket item like a stroller instead of buying lots of small things

The newborn phase is intense but fleeting. The right gift can make those early days a little smoother, a little more comfortable, and a lot more enjoyable for the whole family.
