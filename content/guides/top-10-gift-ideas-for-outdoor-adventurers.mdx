## Choosing the Perfect Gift for Outdoor Adventurers

Match the gift to their adventure style:

- **Backpackers**: Sea to Summit pillow, Osprey Stuff Pack, and Nalgene focus on ultralight performance
- **Car campers**: Stanley thermos, ENO hammock, and Kelty blanket maximize comfort at camp
- **Backcountry explorers**: Garmin inReach, Black Diamond headlamp, and Leatherman provide safety and utility
- **Casual outdoors lovers**: Kelty blanket, Nalgene, and UCO matches are affordable, universally useful gifts

## Pro Tips for Gifting Outdoor Adventurers

1. **Don't buy their big-ticket gear**: Tents, sleeping bags, and packs are deeply personal choices—let them pick their own
2. **Accessories are always welcome**: Headlamps, bottles, blankets, and tools work with any setup
3. **Think about consumables**: Stormproof matches, water purification tablets, and fuel canisters are always needed
4. **Check what they already have**: Outdoor enthusiasts accumulate gear—a quick peek at their setup prevents duplicates
5. **REI gift cards are gold**: When in doubt, let them choose at the outdoor store they already love

The best outdoor gifts are the ones that earn a permanent spot in someone's pack—gear that's so useful it goes on every trip, rain or shine.
