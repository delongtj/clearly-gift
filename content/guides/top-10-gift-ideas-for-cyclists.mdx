## Choosing the Perfect Gift for Cyclists

When selecting gifts for the cyclist in your life, consider their riding style:

- **Road racers**: Garmin Edge, Castelli bib shorts, and Silca pump support their competitive edge
- **Commuters**: Lights, a strong lock, and a saddle bag are daily essentials
- **Indoor trainers**: The KICKR SNAP and a Garmin computer keep them motivated all winter
- **Casual weekend riders**: Pearl Izumi jersey, CamelBak bottle, and Park Tool multi-tool make every ride more enjoyable

## Pro Tips for Gifting Cyclists

1. **Check sizing carefully**: Cycling apparel runs tight—when in doubt, size up or gift a store credit
2. **Don't buy bikes or components**: Compatibility is incredibly specific—let them choose their own frames, wheels, and drivetrains
3. **Accessories are always welcome**: Bottles, tools, lights, and bags work with any bike setup
4. **Think about safety**: Lights and locks are gifts that could literally save their life or their bike
5. **Gift cards work**: REI, Competitive Cyclist, or their local bike shop gift cards let them choose exactly what fits their setup

The best cycling gifts are the ones that get riders out the door more often, keep them safer on the road, and make every mile more enjoyable.

*Ride safe, and happy gifting!* 🚲
