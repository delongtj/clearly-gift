## Choosing the Perfect Gift for Teenagers

When buying for teens, keep these principles in mind:

- **Brand matters**: Teens are brand-aware. Beats, Stanley, Hydro Flask, Fjallraven, and Polaroid carry social currency that generic alternatives don't
- **Self-expression wins**: Gifts that let teens show their personality—camera, phone accessories, customizable items—always land well
- **Tech is safe territory**: Wireless earbuds, speakers, and cameras are universally appreciated across teen interests
- **Don't guess on clothing**: Unless you know their exact style, stick to accessories and experiences

## Pro Tips for Gifting Teenagers

1. **Ask their friends**: If you're unsure what they want, their friends know better than any gift guide
2. **Gift cards aren't lazy**: Teens genuinely prefer choosing their own clothes, games, and accessories—a gift card to their favorite store shows you respect their taste
3. **Pair a small gift with a big one**: A PopSocket + eShop card, or an Instax camera + extra film packs, feels more thoughtful than a single item
4. **Check their phone model**: If buying tech accessories (PopSocket MagSafe, phone case), make sure it's compatible with their specific phone
5. **Don't try to be cool**: Buy what they actually want, not what you think makes you seem in-touch with youth culture

The best gifts for teenagers are the ones that make their daily life a little more fun, a little more expressive, and a little more connected to the people and things they care about.
