## Choosing the Perfect Gift for College Students

When buying for students, think about their daily life:

- **Dorm dwellers**: K-Mini, Echo Dot, and Roku Express transform a small space into a comfortable home
- **Commuters**: AirPods, Hydro Flask, and MagGo portable charger survive long days on campus
- **Study warriors**: Noise-cancelling headphones, Moleskine notebook, and eye mask support marathon sessions
- **Social students**: Bose speaker and streaming player make their space the hangout spot

## Pro Tips for Gifting College Students

1. **Compact wins**: Dorm rooms are tiny—gifts that take up minimal space are always appreciated
2. **Durability matters**: College life is rough on possessions. Choose items that can survive being tossed in a backpack daily
3. **Think about what they won't buy themselves**: Students will buy ramen but not a good notebook. They'll buy cheap earbuds but not quality headphones. Fill those gaps
4. **Gift cards are great**: If you're not sure what they need, an Amazon or Target gift card lets them buy exactly what's missing from their setup
5. **Include consumables**: K-Cup pods, a Hydro Flask boot, or extra charging cables make great add-ons to a main gift

The best college gifts are the ones that make the student experience a little more comfortable, a little more productive, and a lot more enjoyable.
