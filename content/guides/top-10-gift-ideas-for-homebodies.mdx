## Pro Tips for Gifting Homebodies

1. **Quality over novelty**: Homebodies use their favorite items daily—invest in quality they'll appreciate for years, not gimmicks that get used once
2. **Scent is personal**: If gifting candles, stick to universally appealing scents like Baies or Santal 26 rather than niche or polarizing fragrances
3. **Check sizes for slippers and sheets**: Getting the wrong size for slippers or sheets diminishes the gift experience significantly
4. **Pair small with large**: A Fellow Carter mug + a bag of specialty coffee, or a Kindle + a gift card for books, creates a complete experience
5. **Presentation matters at home**: These are gifts for people who appreciate aesthetics—wrap them beautifully

The best homebody gifts aren't about keeping someone inside—they're about making the time they choose to spend at home as comfortable, cozy, and enjoyable as possible.
